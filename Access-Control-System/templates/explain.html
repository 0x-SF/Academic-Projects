01 What is Boolean Algebra?

Boolean Algebra, developed by George Boole in 1854, is a branch of mathematics that operates on binary values: True (1) and False (0). Every decision a computer makes ultimately reduces to Boolean operations. In an Access Control System, it determines who gets in — and who doesn't.
